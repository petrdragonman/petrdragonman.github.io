iRemember iOS app


SUPPORT

iRemember is a simple iOS app to store all of your login or any similar details securely into your personal iCloud account.

All the entry are searchable from the search bar on the home page. You can create a new Item, you can delete an item and also you can update an item.

To create a new entry select the plus button in the right top corner. Make all the entries and press enter to submit the new item. The tile field can not be empty.

To delete an item just swipe right to left.

To update an item select the item from the home screen amd the detail screen will display. Press enter to confirm the changes.
